COMMON QUESTIONS

How many alerts should I expect?
Zero to two per week, on average. This is intentional. Alert fatigue research demonstrates that high-frequency systems produce worse response behavior than sparse, high-confidence ones. When you receive a WWsiren alert, it means something genuinely significant has been classified — not that our system is filling your inbox.
Is WWsiren an official emergency service?
No. WWsiren is a private subscription service. It is not affiliated with any government, military, or emergency organization. Always verify significant information through official channels before taking action. The full scope of the service is described in our Terms.
How does the AI decide what to alert me about?
Events are evaluated against cascade potential, geographic scope, response-time sensitivity, and verification signal density. Only events meeting threshold criteria across multiple dimensions trigger alerts. The complete methodology is in The Science.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of cancellation. Complete details in our Privacy Policy.
