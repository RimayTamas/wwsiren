COOKIES AND TRACKING

WWsiren deploys no tracking cookies, advertising pixels, analytics scripts, or cross-site behavioral monitoring technologies of any kind. The absence of a cookie consent banner on this site is not an oversight — it reflects that we have not deployed the categories of tracking that legally require one under GDPR and ePrivacy Directive requirements.

The only browser storage used is functional session state. It is never transmitted to any external server and is automatically cleared when you close your browser.

POLICY CHANGES

Material changes — to what data we collect, why, who sees it, or what rights you have — will be communicated via direct email to every active subscriber at least 30 days before taking effect, summarized in plain language. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

Non-material changes (typographic corrections, contact detail updates, clarifications that do not alter substance) may be made without advance notice and are reflected in the version date above.
